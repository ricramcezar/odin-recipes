Pumpkin Mousse

Description

This pumpkin mousse really has it all: it feels fancy thanks to its rich creaminess, but it couldn’t be easier to make. There’s no cooking involved, it only requires two ingredients, and it comes together in 10 minutes or less. It also feeds a small crowd.

Ingredients

  • 1 (29 to 30-ounce) can (3 cups) pumpkin pie filling
  • 1 1/2 cups (354 ml) heavy whipping cream, cold

Optional garnishes:

  • 1/4 cup prepared whipped cream
  • 1 tablespoon pumpkin seeds
  • Ground cinnamon, for dusting
  • 1 pinch sea salt flakes

Steps

  1. Strain the pumpkin pie filling
  2. Meanwhile, whip the cream
  3. Fold the mousse together
  4. Chill, refrigerate and serve
